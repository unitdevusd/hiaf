Unit Storage cares about your privacy. For this reason, we collect and use personal information only as needed to deliver services, and to communicate with you about the same, or as you have requested (collectively, our “Services”). Your personal information includes information such as:

  • Name

We collect information related to your transactions on the Unit Service, including the date and time, the amount charged, and other related details.

We collect information about how you and other site visitors interact with and use the Unit Services, such as the pages or other content you view, your searches for listings, bookings that you have made, and other actions you take on our Service. When you interact with our Services, we collect log information, even if you have not created a Unit account. Log information may include details about how you use the Services (including links to third party services), access dates and times, device IP addresses, app features or pages viewed, type of browser, device information, app crashes or other system activity, and the third-party site or services you were using before interacting with our services.

Cookies and Other Related Technologies

The Unit Services use third-party cookies, web beacons, and other related technologies. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your browser or device that can perform functions such as recognize your browser and store your preferences. These technologies provide valuable information on how you use our Services and they enable us to enhance your user experience.

Most browsers automatically allow cookies, however you can choose whether or not to accept cookies by modifying your browser or device settings. Cookies are an important part of our Services’ functionality and disabling cookies may affect how you view or interact with our services.

Flash cookies are different from browser cookies and cannot be disabled via your browser settings. For more information on disabling flash cookies you can visit https://helpx.adobe.com/flash-player/kb/disable-local-shared-objects-flash.html ..
